Our Programme

Zambia Ophans and Vulnerable Children Trust through its projects has selected the needs of children who are Aids orphans or Aids infected, as a central focus. Our current geographical focus is sub-Sahara Africa particularly in Zambia were we work from. Our organisation is working to address this crisis through the following programmes and activities -on the copperbelt region of Zambia.

DIRECT RELIEF

Provision of free education, food medication to the orphaned and vulnerable children at centers.

To give care and support to people and children living with HIV/AIDS

To provide an alternative nursing and psycho social care and counseling to children

To provide school material, such as books, uniforms, clothes and medical fees for sick children and pay school fees for OVC children in government schools.

Promotion of income generating activities at our various centers for children

Food relief programmes for orphaned / vulnerable children in rural areas of Zambia

To provide clean water to the children at our centers and surrounding community.

Lifeline Projects - for people living with HIV/AIDS

Provision of correct information and educate people on the danger of contracting HIV/AIDS.

Power Point Presentations, and workshops seminars on HIV /AIDS

Provision of food, medication to the terminally ill people living with HIV/AIDS -who are bed ridden.

To create open discussions around attitudes and values which influences stigma and discourage discrimination

To help with transportation of HIV/ AIDS terminally ill patients / children helped by Calvarian Ministries to and from hospital for medication

Helping with funerals and burial arrangement of terminally ill people, who die and were helped by Calvarian ministries-world mission in medical expenses on programme.

Hope for Children in Need

Through OVC School's Children care

Provision of advocacy for orphans including legal protection.

Provision of information on orphan/ children and the rights of the orphans / vulnerable children.

Provision and arrangement of adopting opportunities for orphans.

Promotion of overall community development and psycho social environment of orphans.

Provision of school guardians-who cannot afford the fees

Provision of school requisites, uniforms and shoes for children recognized and helped by our organisation in different government schools

Provision of food, farming implement seeds to families looking after orphans/ vulnerable children who are recognized by our organisation in different rural areas of the country.

We hope that through the help of various cooperating partners and our valued donors, the various indicated mentioned programmes and activities in our organisation will continue be sustained in order to alleviate the suffering of the orphaned children and reduce the impact of HIV/AIDS due to poverty in our country Zambia and Africa.

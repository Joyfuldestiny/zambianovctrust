About Zambia Ophans & Vulnerable Children Trust

Zambia Ophans & Vulnerable Children Trust and its projects was established in 1991, in Kitwe Zambia, as we indicated in our organization’s goal and objectives, our overall goal has always been to demonstrate the love of God sharing what we have with the suffering children and to alleviate the plight of the vulnerable and orphaned children, whose parents have died of HIV/AIDS. Our organization was legally allowed to operate due to its good programmes to the needy children and the poor 1991 even if a ban to register new Christian Organization in the country was in place until 1995 when we were given the registration certificate. .

In 1991, the crisis led to the formation of Calvarian Ministries a Christian NGO which sponsors projects in, Zambia Ophans & Vulnerable Children Trust, Life Line project for People living with HIV/AIDS, Luangwa , Kamfinsa and Masaiti / OVC’s children project. Dedicated to being obedient to the biblical call to look after the orphans, widows and families - who have lost parents to HIV/AIDS in their distress putting hope back and creating a better tomorrow for children in Zambia - Africa. Zambia Ophans & Vulnerable Children Trust through its projects rescues and raises orphans in Zambia helping them achieve healthy, prosperous and fulfilling lives. It nurtures and educates these children, while providing healthy care, food and Aids prevention instruction workshops. To achieve this and in order to help alleviate the many problems orphans are facing, Zambia Ophans & Vulnerable Children Trust with its projects plans to invest or purchase a big farm were we will grow food and other farm produce to help feed the children and educate them.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide long term sustainable development through promoting a health care life style for individuals living with HIV/AIDS, families and a holistic care for the orphaned and vulnerable children through,

  • a) empowering families and house holds in various communities as to respond effectively to the needs of orphaned / vulnerable children in their care.
  • (b) Putting in place, effective intervention strategies such as learning programmes, seminars and workshops on HIV/AIDS in order to mitigate the impact of the scourge of HIV/AIDS among families in communities. To work with other NGOs and ensure support to families for the well being of the orphaned and vulnerable children.

Our Vision

a) Our vision is to meet real needs of real people through becoming a catalyst- dynamic focused NGO that will provide HIV/AIDS awareness to people through abstinence and refraining.
b) To net -work with other NGOs and ensure support to families for the wellbeing of orphans and venerable children.
c) To care and to send to school as many venerable as children as possible
d) To evangelize and spread the Good news of the gospel.
